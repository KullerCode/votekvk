JUST CUT TAXES ALREADY!

Property Taxes in Westchester (and especially the Sound Shore) remain the highest in the United States! The County can afford to lower them. This will lower rents too!

  • Reduce the sky-high mortgage recording tax and real estate transfer taxes.
  • Lower the Sales Tax raised in 2019. Support local businesses and give breaks to first responders and the neediest.

GIVE US OUR FAIR SHARE!

Our District pays more in taxes to NY State and Westchester County than it gets back in services.

  • Oppose MTA payroll tax hikes and the congestion pricing tax.

STOP CON ED'S OUTRAGEOUS RATE HIKES!

Kurt wants to:

  1. Lower high local taxes on Con Ed that inflate our bills
  2. Disgorge excessive profits and executive pay
  3. Stop subsidizing NYC's outdated grid
  4. Revise NY State’s impossible energy mandates (70% carbon-free by 2030)

FIX THE FLOODING PROBLEM FINALLY!

Heavy rains still threaten homes and families. Residents face repair costs and rising flood insurance premiums. Very little has been done over many years to mitigate the risks.

  • Much of Mamaroneck’s flooding comes from storm system neglect and overdevelopment. Your local representatives must answer for this!

PROTECT OUR GREAT QUALITY OF LIFE!

Let’s make our streets safer and keep our schools focused on the right priorities.

  • End "Sanctuary County" games that risk losing $100s of millions in federal aid. Protect our Latin American neighborhoods that enrich our lives.
  • Expand recreational facilities for youth. Continue efforts to clean the Sound.

KEEP NATIONAL POLITICS OUT OF DISTRICT 7!

District 7’s needs don’t match national narratives. Kurt is a proven manager and problem-solver who uses common sense, not ideology!

  • Restore a traditional two-party system of checks and balances to the County Legislature.
